$P(A|B)$
: The probability of $A$ given that $B$ has already occurred
- $P(A, B)$ The joint probability of events $A$ and $B$, the probability that both events $A$ and $B$ occur
$$P(A|B) = {P(A, B) \over P(B)}$$
